Howdy

Musician, film photographer, wine enthusiast, programmer, avid lover of cats. I currently reside in Portland, Oregon by way of Oslo, Norway. Raised in Alabama, I spent a lot of time catching frogs and imagining I were elsewhere. Most of my formative years were spent in a state of escapism playing guitar. This later lead to a career in music - signed to Sub-Pop records as the touring/recording guitarst for Kyle Craft. With a hefty touring schedule, I've built quite an appetite for wanderlust. I'm pursuing programming at Epicodus to facilitate an off-season of creative remote work.
