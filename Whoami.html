Musician, film photographer, wine enthusiast, avid lover of cats. I currently reside in Portland Oregon by way of Oslo, Norway. Raised in Alabama, I spent a lot of my time catching frogs and imagining I were elsewhere. Most of my formative years were spent in a state of escapism playing guitar. This later lead to a career in Music - Signed to Sub Pop records as the touring/recording guitarst for Kyle Craft. With a hefty touring schedule Ive built quite an appitite of wanderlust. Im persuing programming at Epicodus to facilitate an off season of creative remote work.
